Step 1 of 3

What are you using LinkedIn for?

Pick your goal. The AI will analyze every profile differently depending on what you're trying to do.

Step 2 of 3

Set up your profile

The AI uses your profile to personalize every analysis and outreach message. The more detail you add, the more accurate and useful the results.

B2B Sales

Your Ideal Customer Profile (ICP)

Tell the AI which industries you sell into and which to skip. It uses this to rate every prospect as Strong Fit, Partial Fit, or Poor Fit — and automatically flags excluded industries so you don't waste time on bad leads.

B2B Sales

Your Business Profile

Tell the AI what you sell, who you sell to, and what problem you solve. It uses this to write personalized connection requests — subtly referencing your context without ever making a hard pitch. The more specific you are, the better the messages.

B2B Sales

Message Style

Set your default tone and length for AI-generated connection requests and cold messages. You can always override these on a per-profile basis by adding a custom note directly in the panel.

Freelance

Your Personal Profile

Tell the AI about your expertise and services so it can evaluate each contact as a potential client and suggest the most effective outreach angle — always positioning you as a trusted expert, never as a vendor pitching services.

Job Search

Your Job Seeker Profile

The AI uses this to write connection requests that sound natural and professional — not like a job application. Adding your background helps it reference your specific situation when reaching out to recruiters and hiring managers.

Step 3 of 3

Connect the AI & integrations

Add your OpenAI API key to activate the AI. HubSpot is optional — connect it to push LinkedIn contacts straight into your CRM.

OpenAI API Key Required

Needed to run AI analysis on LinkedIn profiles. Your key is stored only in your browser and sent directly to OpenAI — no one else sees it. Get your API key →

Keys start with sk- and are found on the OpenAI platform under API keys.

HubSpot CRM Optional

Push LinkedIn profiles directly into HubSpot as deals — with notes, pipeline stage, and deal owner. You'll need a Private App token from your HubSpot account.

1 Go to app.hubspot.com/private-apps and click Create a private app
2 Name it Husnain LinkedIn Helper, then open the Scopes tab
3
Enable these scopes:
crm.objects.deals.read crm.objects.deals.write crm.objects.notes.write crm.objects.owners.read crm.objects.contacts.write

Step 4 — Optional

LinkedIn Post Creator

Activate the ✍️ Post button on your own LinkedIn profile. The AI will suggest trending topic ideas in your niche, write full posts, and generate matching images — ready to copy and publish.

Content

Your Content Creator Profile

The AI uses this to generate posts that sound like you — specific to your domain, your audience, and your goals on LinkedIn. The more detail you add, the better the content.

B2B Company

Your Company Profile

Enable the 🏢 Company mode in the Post Creator to generate authoritative B2B posts on behalf of your company — targeting clients, stakeholders, and top talent. The AI writes as the company, not as you personally.
